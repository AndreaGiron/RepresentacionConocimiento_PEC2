Abstract

Tourism and Recomendations Ontology

Introduction back to ToC

This ontology aims to formally model the domain of smart tourism, including tourist events, hotel offerings, attractions, and restaurants in various cities. It is designed to structure, analyze, and reuse tourism-related information to support personalized recommendations of destinations and services. The ontology will define key tourism concepts and their relationships, enabling knowledge inference from available data.
